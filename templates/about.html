BYTEsense is a web app developed as an AI machine learning project for Hack the North 2023.

(Developed by Dolev Klein, Savir Singh, Manasvi Bandi, and Nithya Majeti)

What is the purpose of the app?

The reason we developed BYTEsense is so that people can discover and learn more about their allergies and dietary preferences through an engaging and unique interface. Through BYTEsense, people can understand what meals and dishes their system cannot tolerate, or does not prefer. In a world where more than 95% of people have health issues, and little care is put into choosing the right meals, BYTEsense makes it easy remain healthy while you "Taste Life, Byte by Byte!"

How to use the app?

Our app's functionality is relatively straightforward: you create an account, and once you log in, you're presented with two options: after eating a meal, give it a star rating (out of 5), or if you're about to consume a meal, check how the dish will make you feel before actually trying it. Note that in order to benefit the most from our app, we highly recommend you give an honest review (not too extreme) of each meal, and ensure that even if your meal is 5/5, try letting BYTEsense know, just so we know more about your preferences and can serve you better next time :)
